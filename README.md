# Bank-Loan-Portfolio-Risk-Assessment-and-Mitigation-Strategy-using-Tableau
Data-driven analysis of a $79.3M bank loan portfolio using Tableau. Identified critical concentration risks (geographic, credit grade) and proposed a strategic risk mitigation roadmap


Objective:-	To perform a comprehensive risk assessment of a $79.30M loan portfolio, identify key concentration and credit risks, and provide data-backed strategic recommendations to the Executive Risk Committee.

Tools & Data:-	Tableau (Visualization), Financial Data (Loan Amount: $79.30M, Principal: $61.32M, Defaults: $3.89M)

Problem Statement:-	The executive team needed clarity on potential hidden risks, geographical exposure, and credit quality concentration across the entire loan book to prevent material losses.

Key Questions Addressed	
List the main analytical questions: 
1. What is the portfolio's credit risk profile (by Grade)?
2. Where is the highest geographic concentration?
3. What is the status of asset quality realization (Collection vs. Principal)?
